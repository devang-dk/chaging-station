Required Locations of PCSs

  1. 1. At least one charging station must be installed in a 3x3-kilometer grid.
  2. 2. One charging station must be set up every 25 kilometers on highways/roads’ both sides.
  3. 3. For long-range and heavy EVs, one Fast Charging Station should also be available on each side of the highways.
  4. 4. In cities, charging stations should be located within Transport Nagars and bus depots.
  5. 5. The appropriate authorities (State/Central/UTs) can also give priority to the current retail outlets (ROs) of Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) in the permission to install of PCSs.
